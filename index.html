About me

Hello I'm Azlee, and this is my project website for the Shift Click 'Introduction to Coding' course.

I have previously worked as a musician and sound engineer and now, due to loss of work to the COVID-19 pandemic, I am looking to transition into a career as a software engineer.

I have previous experience working with code in musical and artistic contexts, working with software such as Max/MSP, Pure Data and Processing for various projects for gigs and art galleries. From these experiences I have developed a love for the process of software development - deconstructing a vision down into programmable elements and then progressively building these elements up into the final work.

I am currently training as a full-stack web developer. So far I have a grounding in HTML, CSS, and Javascipt. I am comfortable using technologies such as VS Code, Github and the command-line terminal.
